Pricing FAQ

Is there a free trial? +

Yes. Standard plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. Starter is free forever with no trial period.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or churches? +

Yes. Churches and registered nonprofits can contact us for a 25% discount on any paid plan. Email hello@storytrackr.app with proof of nonprofit status.

What happens if I go over my student limit? +

You'll be prompted to upgrade. You won't lose any data — you just won't be able to add new students until you upgrade or remove inactive ones.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes. Month-to-month with no contracts. Cancel anytime from your billing settings and your plan continues through the end of the billing period.

Can I export my data if I leave? +

Absolutely. Your data belongs to you. You can export all student and interaction data as CSV or JSON at any time from Settings → Export.
